We chose very subtle, calm and pastel like colours. The palette is very colourful and matches the cute illustrative theme we are going for with our app. The brown colours go well with our dog theme, while the Asparagus green, Columbia blue, and Melon pink go well with the browns. We chose these colours because they create a light and happy feeling just like dogs do!
Background
The Columbia Blue is used as a sky background accompanied by clouds and rain drops to convey a fun theme of raining cats and dogs.
Illustrations & Text
The Caput Mortuum and Rosy Brown are used as colours for dog illustrations. The Caput Mortuum can also be used as a text colour to create good contrast between the text and the background colour which is the very light Columbia Blue.
Buttons & Components
The Melon and Asparagus colours are used as accent colours and are used more prevalently through components such as buttons, additional illustrations, header text, card placeholders for text and much more.
