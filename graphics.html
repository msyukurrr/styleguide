Imagery, Icons and Graphics

Graphics
We wanted the style of graphics to be appealing to our users. Though they are cute, the content of our infographic is still projected.
Sizing
The size of our images and graphics will be approximately 100px by 100px.
Animations
We plan on making some elements of our imagery to move around. For example, the tennis ball will be jumping from one side to another to make inforgraphic more engaging and dynamic than static. Other functionalities include: clicking buttons that lead to more information, clicking onto different options and being able to see what user have clicked on (change of colour or added stroke); etc.
Details
We put rounded corners on all our containers. The rounded corners are a lot easier on the eyes than sharp edges.
Graphics References
All graphics seen on screen were designed and drawn by us!
